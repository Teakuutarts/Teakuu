About Teakuu

 •  Teakuu was founded back in 2020 by Me! at the age of 15 as a small unofficial and still unofficial business school project.

I had then repurposed it for a Minecraft gaming platform which got separated to TeakuuMC which left me with other ideas for Teakuu.

Since then, Teakuu has expanded far beyond what it used to be, so here are the basics of everything about Teakuu & what we'd like to accomplish!

Teakuu strives to be a fully fledged social media app primarily focused on creating user entertainment, servers, and communities all while offering many other tools.
